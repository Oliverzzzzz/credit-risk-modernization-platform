# Business Summary

This project demonstrates how a financial institution could modernize credit risk assessment with explainable machine learning and API-based decision support.

## Modernization Value

- Faster underwriting support through real-time scoring.
- More transparent decisions through reason codes and explainability.
- Better operational control through logging and monitoring.
- More maintainable implementation through modular Python services.
- More repeatable deployment through Docker and CI/CD.

## Governance Position

The platform is a simulation and should not be represented as a regulated production credit approval system. It is intended to show enterprise engineering judgment, model governance awareness, and practical AI modernization design.
